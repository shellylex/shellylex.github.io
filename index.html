Music has always been a part of my life, whether it was from my parents, or by listening to the stuff playing through the TV. Around my 10th birthday, I discovered EDM, and quickly got interested in "harder" music genres such as DnB, Dubstep (Brostep), and Hardmusic subgenres.

And in 2019 my mom offered me the DJ controller I wanted and started practicing DJing because I wanted to make my own DJ mixes for me (quite egoistic lol). In January/February of 2020 I started to make music with FL Studio, which I started using quite often (almost daily) and the lockdown in France pushed me even more down this way.
I started by making beats, then I made DnB where I learned a lot about sound design on youtube, and I ended up making Techno and Jungle, as well as Boom Bap sometimes.

I'm super influenced by 90's electronic music and how it really defines the roots of most of the things we hear nowadays in any music genres.
